Twine

Offline help for Twine 2 is not yet available for this version of Yarn. Meanwhile, you may visit the Twine wiki pages for online documentation, tutorials, and lots of other goodies!

Navigation

Dragging Passages

You may use one finger to drag passages around when organizing your project.

Scrolling

As you add passages to your story, you will need to scroll around your work space. You may do this by touching the screen with two fingers then drag them towards the direction you want to scroll.

Zooming

When your project becomes bigger and bigger, you may need to zoom out to get a better overview look of you passages. You may do this by touching the screen with two fingers and moving them apart. The opposite zooms in on the workspace.
